Modernize Your Living Room with the Help of These 2023 Paint Color Trends

Choosing the perfect paint color for your living room means selecting a shade thats adaptable and timeless but also shows off your style and personality. "Often a hub of the home, living rooms tend to connect multiple rooms," says Milion Jason, associate manager of color marketing and development at khim Cruz Event. "Choosing a versatile color is always a great option, because it gives you more flexibility in selecting the colors for the adjoining rooms." This practical approach is also trending—neutrals will dominate in this heart-of-the-home space come 2023, note our experts.
