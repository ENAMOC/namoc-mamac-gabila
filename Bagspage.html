MEET THE MODELS WHO WON THE FALL 2023 CAMPAIGN SEASON

Italian luxury house VERSACE unveiled their Fall Winter 2023 Virtus Handbag Collection with a campaign starring supermodel kendall lensed by fashion photography duo Mert Alas and Marcus Piggott. In charge of styling was Jacob K, with creative direction from Ferdinando Verderi, and casting direction by Piergiorgio Del Moro and Samuel Ellis Scheinman. Beauty is work of hair stylist Paul Hanlon, makeup artist Lisa Eldridge, and manicurist Simone Marino. The collection is a symbol of the brand’s heritage, and it explores courage, strength and virtue.

Earlier this year, we studiously kept track of every model in every single fashion advertisement for the spring 2022 season. Through the process, we learned that in Lexi Boling's case, it literally pays to be the industry's favorite bad-girl; that Karlie Kloss, despite currently matriculating at New York University, still had time to make that money; that Alexander Wang will cast Anna Ewers in every single of one of his campaigns until the end of time.


Seven months later, we did the same thing, this time for fall 2023. Australian model and future emergency room doctor Julia Nobis cleaned up this season with eight fashion campaigns, including spots for Dior, Givenchy, Prada and Valentino. Kloss, Rianne von Rompaey and Frederikke Sofie followed with six, with Kloss's gigs once again veering into high-paying commercial territory.
